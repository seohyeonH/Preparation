In South Korea, you can report wage arrears to the Ministry of Employment and Labor. There are regional labor offices across the country where grievances can be lodged. You could also use the Labor Law Counseling Service provided by the Ministry. Additionally, the Ministry operates a 24-hour call center, so you can call in to report your case. You may also report online via the Ministry of Employment and Labor's e-people website. Please make sure to have necessary documents handy to support your claim.
