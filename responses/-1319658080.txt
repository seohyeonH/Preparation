In South Korea, if you have a problem at work that cannot be resolved through your employer, you could reach out to the Ministry of Employment and Labor through their hotline at 1350, or to the Korea Industrial Relations Commission at (02) 2104-2000. If you face workplace discrimination, you may contact the National Human Rights Commission of Korea at (02) 2113-3100. If the issue involves foreigners/expats, reaching out to the Ministry of Justice's Immigrant Affairs Division at 1345 might be helpful. Make sure to seek professional advice appropriate for your specific situation.
